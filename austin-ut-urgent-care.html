MedSpring UT Area

MedSpring Urgent Care near the University of Texas at Austin has a doctor on-site from 9am-9pm every day, including weekends and holidays. Located on the Drag (or Guadalupe St.) across from the Ransom Center, our UT Austin Urgent Care offers students and non-students alike treatment for routine illnesses, infections and other minor emergencies.
