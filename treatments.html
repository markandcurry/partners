What We Treat

We provide treatment for a wide range of injuries and illnesses for adults and children over 12 months of age. A physician is on-site every day, and our friendly medical staff are there every day - even evenings, weekends and holidays - to help you get back to what matters. Here is just a small list of injuries and illnesses we can treat and services we offer.

Types of Injuries We Treat

  • Cuts
    Serious open wounds through the skin or if the bleeding won't stop
  • Bites
    Animal, reptile or human bites that need extra care
  • Breaks
    Broken or fractured bones may require x-ray and specific treatment in order to heal
  • Burns
    Damage to the body caused by heat, chemicals, electricity, sunlight or radiation
  • Ingrown Nails
    Toe nails ingrown into the skin might require expertise
  • Back Pains
    There are many causes for upper or lower back pain may require treatment
  • Punctures Wounds
    Deep wounds caused by something piercing the skin
  • Scrapes
    Wounds that rub or tear off the skin can sometimes be serious or get infected
  • Sprains and Strains
    Stretched or torn ligaments, muscles or tendons
